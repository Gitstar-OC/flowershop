---
title: 'How adding audio ads can boost your cannabis advertising campaign.'
date: 'Jan 1, 2023'
---

Cannabis advertising can be a challenging landscape due to the various laws and regulations surrounding the industry. However, programmatic audio advertising offers a unique opportunity for cannabis brands to reach their target audience in a highly effective and compliant way.

One of the main benefits of programmatic audio advertising is the ability to target specific demographics and genres using data from audio partners. This allows cannabis brands to reach a specific audience that is interested in or likely to purchase their products. Audio ads also have a high recall rate, meaning that listeners are more likely to remember and engage with the brand. This is especially important for cannabis brands looking to build awareness and trust with their target audience.

In addition to targeting specific demographics, programmatic audio advertising allows cannabis brands to reach their audience in an engaged and receptive state of mind. Audio ads are emotionally engaging and capture listeners’ attention in a way that is difficult to achieve with other types of advertising. This makes them the perfect medium for conveying brand personality and building brand awareness.

In addition to standalone campaigns, programmatic audio advertising can also be used in conjunction with other channels to further boost your marketing efforts. Retarget listeners with native or display ads, and watch your brand awareness and conversions soar. And with the ability to track and optimize your campaigns using metrics like reach, frequency, and engagement, you’ll be able to fine-tune your strategy for maximum success.

Don’t let your competitors get ahead – start leveraging the power of programmatic audio advertising today!
