---
title: 'Make contextual targeting key to your cannabis brand’s success'
date: 'Jan 1, 2023'
---

Cannabis advertising can be a complex and challenging landscape due to the various laws and regulations surrounding the industry. However, contextual targeting is an innovative strategy that can help cannabis brands reach their target audience in a highly effective and compliant way.

Contextual targeting allows cannabis brands to target online shoppers based on the content they are consuming, ensuring that they are captured in the right moments. This is especially valuable in an unpredictable market, as buying habits may shift and traditional consumer behaviors may no longer hold true. By understanding the demographics and buying behaviors of their target audience, cannabis brands can use in-context phrases related to the interests and milestones of their audience to target content that will be most relevant to them.

One of the main benefits of contextual targeting for cannabis brands is the ability to reach consumers in an engaged and receptive state of mind. Cannabis products and services are often used for specific purposes or to address certain issues, so targeting content related to those purposes or issues can help capture users who are actively seeking out information about cannabis products. This can be especially effective during the holiday season, when consumers are more open to exploring new products and services.

However, it’s important to note that cannabis advertising is subject to strict laws and regulations, and brands must ensure that they are compliant with all applicable laws. This includes ensuring that ads are only shown to users who are of legal age to purchase cannabis products and only in states where cannabis is legal.

Overall, contextual targeting is a powerful and compliant way for cannabis brands to reach their target audience and drive awareness and sales. By following best practices and staying up-to-date on the latest laws and regulations, cannabis brands can effectively leverage this strategy to achieve their marketing goals.
